Tacos al Pastor

Description

These tacos are not only famous for their amazing taste but also for how easy they are to prepare.

All you need is a slow cooker and in no time you will have savory tacos to enjoy with your family.

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder (3 lb)
  • Pineapple (4 spears)
  • Guajillo chiles (6)
  • Chile de arbol (1)
  • Garlic (5 cloves)
  • White onion (1/4)
  • Whole black peppercorns (10)
  • Oregano (1 teaspoon)
  • Cumin (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Ground cloves(1/4 teaspoon)
  • Ground ginger (1/4 teaspoon)
  • Kosher salt (1 1/2 teaspoon)
  • Apple cider vinegar (1/4 cup)
  • Orange juice (1/4 cup)
